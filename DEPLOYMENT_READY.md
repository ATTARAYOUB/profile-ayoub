### **vs PDF:**
- ✅ **Instant updates** - Edit HTML, redeploy, done
- ✅ **Always latest version** - No old PDFs floating around
- ✅ **Print-to-PDF** - Users can save their own copy
- ✅ **Responsive** - Works on mobile, tablet, desktop
- ✅ **Interactive** - Clickable links work
- ✅ **Trackable** - Can add analytics if needed
- ✅ **Professional** - Shows technical skills

- ✅ **Country-specific** - Tailored messaging
- ✅ **Relevant codes** - NOC/ANZSCO/Tech Visa

- ✅ **Higher conversion** - Speaks directly to recruiters
